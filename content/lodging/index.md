We have a preferential rate agreement (starting at $63+tax) with the ZUM MTL summer student hostel, located on Université de Montréal campus.<br><br>
Here is the link to the booking page intended to International conference on Timbre 2026 participants: [https://www.zumauberge.ca/en/ict2026](https://www.zumauberge.ca/en/ict2026).<br><br>
The eligible period of stay is from June 30, 2026 to July 9, 2026  You can book your stay within this period, with a **minimum of 3 nights**. <br><br>
The double rooms can be booked only after April 1st.<br><br>
Note that Montréal weather can be very hot in the summer and that these rooms have no air conditioning but we were assured that they are well ventilated and equipped with portable fans.
